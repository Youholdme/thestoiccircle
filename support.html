Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Stoic Circle?

The Stoic Circle is your personal Stoic companion app. It combines the wisdom of three great Stoic philosophers – Marcus Aurelius, Seneca, and Epictetus – with daily routines, journaling, exercises, and progress tracking to help you live a more mindful life.

Is The Stoic Circle a replacement for therapy?

No. The Stoic Circle is designed for personal growth and philosophical reflection. It does not provide medical, psychological, or therapeutic advice. If you are experiencing mental health issues, please consult a qualified professional.

How does the AI chat work?

The AI chat uses Anthropic's Claude to generate responses in the style of the Stoic philosophers. Your conversations are processed securely and are not used to train AI models.

Which languages are supported?

The app is available in English, German, French, and Spanish. You can change the language in Settings at any time.

How do I reset the app?

You can delete and reinstall the app from the App Store. This will reset all local data including your journal entries and progress.

How do I report a bug?

Please send us an email at youhold@gmx.de with a description of the issue and, if possible, a screenshot. We appreciate your feedback!
